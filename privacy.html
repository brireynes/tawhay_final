We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, contact number, delivery details, and order information when you register, place an order, or contact us through the website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to support a better shopping experience, including:

3. Data Protection

Tawhay Wellness takes reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. While this student project may use simulated systems, we still present information responsibly and securely.

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Information may only be used for order handling, customer support, and delivery-related processes within the scope of the website experience.

Our site may use basic browser storage or cookies to improve usability, remember cart activity, and maintain smoother navigation across pages.

6. Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain personal information, but doing so may limit access to some website features such as account registration, checkout, or delivery details.

7. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page and reach out to Tawhay Wellness support.
