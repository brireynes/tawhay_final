Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers about shopping, ordering, delivery, and the Tawhay Wellness experience.

What is Tawhay Wellness?

Tawhay Wellness is a reflective wellness brand centered on peace, clarity, and intentional living through thoughtfully presented products and calming digital experiences.

How do I place an order?

Browse the shop, open a product page, choose your preferred variation and quantity, then add the item to your cart before proceeding to checkout.

Can I edit the quantity of items in my cart?

Yes. The cart page is designed to let users adjust item quantities and view updated subtotal and total price calculations.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes. Delivery information is presented as part of the website flow, including shipping details and estimated timeframes depending on the order setup.

What payment methods are available?

Payment methods may be simulated as part of the student project, but the checkout flow is designed to reflect a realistic e-commerce experience with multiple options.

Can I create an account?

Yes. Users can register and log in to access account-related features and improve the overall shopping experience.

Can I return a product?

Return requests are subject to the Return and Refund Policy presented on the site. Please review that page for process details and conditions.

How do I contact Tawhay Wellness?

You may contact us through the Contact page using the provided email, phone, or message form details available on the website.
